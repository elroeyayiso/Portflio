Project 2: iSchool Study Abroad Ethiopia | Researcher

During this research project in Ethiopia, I helped lead a study aimed at improving the long wait times people face with public transportation. We interviewed commuters, collected survey data, and observed the daily challenges people experience with buses. Based on this research, we proposed a real-time bus tracking app that would allow commuters to see when their bus is coming. Over 75% of the people we surveyed supported the idea. This project taught me a lot about working together as a team and using data to solve real problems.

Visualizing COVID-19 Health Data

Duration: April 2023 - May 2023
Description:
In this project, I am working on developing interactive visualizations that help present COVID-19 health data in a clear and accessible way. Using Processing, JavaScript, and Java, I create graphics that show how users understand and interact with the data. Additionally, I co-designed health visuals that are culturally relevant, using generative AI to ensure they align with the community's values. Based on insights from over 50 participants, we are building a website with Java, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS to showcase these visuals and present our findings on how to effectively communicate health information.

Duration: January 2023 - March 2023
Description:
